ADVOCACY BACKGROUND

It all began during unit meet. Me and my PSHS teammates passed by a blood donation drive
where there was a commotion within the organizers. It turned out that the blood was limited and
lacking the minimum volunteers they weren't able to donate blood to those in need. After winning
Unit meet and qualifying for DECAA we decided to use our current winnings and popularity to support
these drives. But as it turns out more than just 1 donation drive was in dire need of
help. So were people who lacked budget to afford to deliver a baby.

This led to many deaths and mistakes. Because of this my teammates and I decided to start
a funding event which would have us volunteering to organize the event. But sadly we failed
to organize the event due to budget and time constraints. Being PISAY scholars we stayed resilient
and instead decided to create a web page which would reach out to spread awareness and present
methods how to help. Without spending money and allowing everyone to help in their respective homes.
The very website you're reading now. We hope to attract adolescents like us to be able to support
this SDG and bring more people to help using different methods.




ABOUT THE AUTHOR

My name is Carlos Miguel Rosello Buenaventura. An adolescent. I play football and am a son of
a doctor. I have seen the struggles of my father as a doctor and the struggles of patients who
can't afford to pay for their medicines and check ups. This is why I chose this advocacy.
To help those in need. To help those who can't help themselves. To help those who are suffering.



Because I believe that health is wealth.And I want to share this wealth with everyone. I am
also a PSHS scholar who serves the nation. And the people being under the nation it is my mission
and my personal goal to help and ensure their good health and well-being. But as of now my capability
has only reached this website. I hope you enjoy!
